A workshop participant explores the new Agroclimate Viewer and Planner App during an information session for growers presented by Abhilash Chandel.
Precision Ag Specialist Abhilash Chandel and his team have developed the Agroclimate Viewer and Planner App (AgroVAP), a free, web-based tool to help growers use satellite and weather data to optimize irrigation, fertilization, and harvest decisions.
